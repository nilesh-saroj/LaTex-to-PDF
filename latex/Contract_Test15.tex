\section{Services}

Anantam HR will provide permanent placement recruitment services to the Client, including candidate sourcing, screening, and interview coordination. Reference checks are available at the Client's discretion for an additional fee.

\section{Charges}

The recruitment fee is \textbf{8.33\% of the candidate's annual CTC}. Background verification (BGV), GST, and other specific requirements will be charged separately where applicable.

\section{Replacement Guarantee}

If a candidate placed by \textbf{Anantam HR Ltd} resigns within the applicable replacement period from their \textbf{joining date}, the Client will be entitled to a \textbf{replacement candidate at no additional cost}.

The replacement guarantee period is defined as follows:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.2cm]
\item \textbf{Up to 3 LPA CTC} - 1 Month replacement period
\item \textbf{Up to 9 LPA CTC} - 2 Months replacement period
\item \textbf{Above 9 LPA CTC} - 3 Months replacement period
\end{itemize}

To avail this guarantee, the Client must notify Anantam HR Ltd in writing within \textbf{7 days} of the candidate's resignation.

This guarantee is limited to a \textbf{maximum of three replacements} per original placement and does not apply if:

\begin{itemize}
\item The candidate is terminated by the Client
\item The role, location, or salary is changed after hiring
\end{itemize}

\section{Payment Terms}

This agreement remains valid from the execution date until termination.

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{50\% payment} is due within 30 days of the invoice date
\item Remaining \textbf{50\% payment} is due within the next 15 days
\item Payments must be made via \textbf{bank transfer only}. Overdue payments may incur a \textbf{2\% monthly interest}.
\item All applicable taxes including GST will be payable by the Client.
\item Payment confirmation and tax certificates may be sent to: \href{mailto:manila.bhatia@anantamhr.com}{manila.bhatia@anantamhr.com}
\end{itemize}

\section{Detailed Scope of Work}

\textbf{Responsibilities of Anantam HR}

\begin{itemize}
\item Obtain candidate consent before presenting profiles
\item Present only relevant and suitable candidates
\item Inform the Client of any known inaccuracies in candidate information
\item Arrange interviews with shortlisted candidates
\item Refrain from poaching from the Client during the contract period
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Responsibilities of the Client}

\begin{itemize}
\item Provide written confirmation of recruitment requirements
\item Share complete job descriptions and candidate expectations
\item Provide feedback or schedule interviews within 48 working hours
\item Inform Anantam HR Ltd immediately of hiring decisions
\end{itemize}

\section{Duplicate Profile Clause}

If the Client identifies a duplicate candidate profile, verifiable proof must be provided within \textbf{48 hours}, including the original email or document reference.

\section{Data Protection Compliance}

\begin{itemize}
\item Anantam HR Ltd will comply with \textbf{DPD 2023 regulations} in the handling of personal data.

\item Both parties agree to maintain relevant data records for \textbf{12 months} for compliance and audit purposes.
\end{itemize}
